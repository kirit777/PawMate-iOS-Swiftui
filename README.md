# PawMate-iOS-Swiftui

PawMate

Your complete pet companion for safe walks, nearby pet services, lost pet alerts, and pet care management.


PawMate helps pet owners discover pet-friendly places, create and save safe walking routes, receive and share lost pet alerts, manage pet care reminders, and explore nearby vets, pet shops, parks, grooming centers, and shelters.

The app combines location-based pet services with smart pet management tools in one beautiful and easy-to-use experience. Whether you're planning a daily walk, searching for a nearby veterinary clinic, tracking important pet care schedules, or helping reunite lost pets with their families, PawMate gives pet owners everything they need in a single app.

Designed with a modern map-first experience, PawMate makes pet ownership safer, easier, and more connected.
